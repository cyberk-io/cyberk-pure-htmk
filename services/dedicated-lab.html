Frequently Asked Questions

001/What is a Dedicated Lab?▾
A Dedicated Lab is a long-term development team model where Cyberk provides engineers who work exclusively on your product. Unlike project-based outsourcing, the team is fully embedded in your workflows, builds deep product knowledge, and operates as an extension of your in-house team.
002/How quickly can you set up a Dedicated Lab team?▾
We can ramp up a team in as little as 2-5 days for standard roles. For specialized positions, it typically takes 1-2 weeks. Our bench of pre-vetted engineers means you don't have to wait through lengthy recruitment cycles.
003/Can I scale the team up or down?▾
Absolutely. The Dedicated Lab model is designed for flexibility. You can scale from 3 to 12+ engineers based on project phases, budget, or priority shifts. We handle all the resourcing logistics so you can focus on product strategy.
004/What roles can you provide?▾
We provide full-stack developers, blockchain engineers, smart contract developers, AI/ML engineers, QA specialists, DevOps engineers, UI/UX designers, and project managers. We build the exact team composition your project needs.
005/How is the Dedicated Lab different from traditional outsourcing?▾
Traditional outsourcing is project-based and transactional. The Dedicated Lab is relationship-based — your team participates in your standups, follows your processes, and builds institutional knowledge. This leads to higher quality, faster velocity, and better alignment with your business goals.

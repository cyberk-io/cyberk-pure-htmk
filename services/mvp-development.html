Frequently Asked Questions

001/What is an MVP and why do I need one?▾
An MVP (Minimum Viable Product) is the simplest version of your product that includes only the core features needed to solve a real problem. It lets you test your idea with real users, gather feedback, and validate demand — before investing heavily in full-scale development.
002/How can Cyberk deliver an MVP in 30 days?▾
We use pre-built architectures, reusable smart contract frameworks, and proven infrastructure patterns. Our team focuses exclusively on Web3 and AI, which means zero ramp-up time. We scope tightly, build iteratively, and ship fast — without cutting corners on quality or security.
003/What technologies do you use for MVP development?▾
We work with modern frameworks and languages including React, Next.js, Node.js, Solidity, Rust, and more. Our tech stack is chosen based on your specific project requirements to ensure scalability, performance, and maintainability.
004/What happens after the MVP is delivered?▾
After delivery, we provide a hypercare period to monitor performance and address any issues. From there, you can iterate based on user feedback, scale the product, or transition to our Dedicated Lab service for ongoing long-term development.
005/Is the pricing really fixed?▾
Yes. We agree on a fixed scope and price before development begins. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges. You know exactly what you're paying for and what you'll receive, giving you full budget predictability.

Types of Smart Contracts

  • Upgradeable Contracts — Proxy patterns (UUPS, Transparent) for safe, seamless upgrades.
  • Smart Account (Account Abstraction) — ERC-4337 compliant wallets with gasless transactions and social recovery.
  • Staking & Yield Contracts — Flexible staking pools, reward distribution, and yield farming mechanisms.
  • DAO & Governance — On-chain governance, voting systems, and treasury management.
  • NFT Contracts — ERC-721, ERC-1155, lazy minting, royalty enforcement, and marketplace integration.
  • Oracle Integration — Chainlink, Pyth, and custom oracle solutions for reliable off-chain data feeds.
  • ERC-20 / ERC-721 Tokens — Standard-compliant token contracts with extensions for vesting, burning, and access control.
  • Layer 2 & Cross-chain — Bridges, rollup contracts, and cross-chain messaging protocols.

Frequently Asked Questions

001/What blockchains do you support for smart contract development?▾
We support all major EVM-compatible chains (Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Mantle) as well as Solana, Aptos, and Sui. We select the optimal chain based on your project's requirements for speed, cost, and ecosystem.
002/How do you ensure smart contract security?▾
Security is built into every step. We follow industry best practices including comprehensive unit and integration testing, formal verification where applicable, and support for third-party audits. We use battle-tested patterns like OpenZeppelin and implement safeguards against common vulnerabilities.
003/Can you upgrade smart contracts after deployment?▾
Yes. We use proxy patterns (UUPS and Transparent Proxy) that allow contracts to be upgraded without losing data or changing the contract address. This ensures your protocol can evolve safely over time.
004/What is the typical timeline for smart contract development?▾
Simple contracts (tokens, basic staking) can be delivered in 1-2 weeks. Complex DeFi protocols or cross-chain solutions typically take 4-8 weeks, including testing and documentation. We scope precisely so there are no surprises.
005/Do you provide post-deployment support?▾
Absolutely. We offer a hypercare period after deployment to monitor contract performance, handle any issues, and assist with upgrades. For ongoing support, our Dedicated Lab service provides a long-term development partnership.
